Software Description

The Voice Translator App is a convenient tool that allows users to convert spoken Chinese into English text. The App is designed to be user-friendly and provides accurate and conversational translations that are easy to understand. Simply speak in Chinese, and the App will quickly translate your words into English, making it ideal for both learning and communication purposes.

Features:

  • Real-time voice-to-text translation from Chinese to English.
  • Accurate and conversational translation output.
  • Simple and intuitive interface for ease of use.

How to Use the App:

  • Open the App and allow access to your microphone.
  • Press the record button and start speaking in Chinese.
  • The App will process your speech and display the English translation.
  • Copy or share the translated text as needed.
